ABSTRACT

  Archives play a vital role in centralizing knowledge for easy access to records, documents, and any other files that contain information. The IC Archive was made exclusively for the students and/or professors of the Institute of Computing. The Mobile Application’s purpose is to have an easier access to research documents relating to projects the students of the Institute of Computing has already made.

  The IC Archive provides a search engine for users to search any particular research documentation they need. The app is only limited to provide Abstracts on Capstones/Thesis, Background Study on Projects, etc.
