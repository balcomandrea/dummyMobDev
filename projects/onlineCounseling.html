Online Guidance Counseling for USeP Students

Develop by: Regalado, Marnela Angela A.,Balcom, Andrea Gail N., Cagape, Reyna Elaine G.
Hermoso Tupas Jr., Nancy Mozo


ABSTRACT

  Mental health is a vital and delicate matter to discuss. It is something that some people have difficulty talking about, especially those who suffer from mental illness. The fear and anxiousness they feel when opening themselves up to others, especially face to face, could and might be nightmare to them. Our project involves tackling into this territory, with the goal of easing the burden of having to talk face to face to someone they hardly know.

  The purpose is to provide a way for students to seek counseling anonymously or not, especially for those students that are too anxious to go directly to the University Guidance and Testing Office. The system functions as a chat where students can either go anonymous or not, depending on their preference. The importance of the system is to help ease the fear and anxiety students feel when they are to seek guidance to the counselors.

1 of 2

  This app is available only for the students of USeP, especially those going through difficult times. Counselors can reply to multiple messages concurrently.
