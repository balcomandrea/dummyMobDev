BOOK HUB

Mary Elisse Gonzales,Mice Dianne Morta, Marnela Angela Regalado, April Grace Vergara
Nancy Mozo


ABSTRACT

  Book Hub is a mobile application that will help users such as students who loves to borrow books this app will help them ease the laborious in finding books if it is available in the library.

  Book Hub’s main is to let users view, see and search for the books with its description.

  Book Hub mobile application is intended to help and assist students who wants to borrow books from the library.
