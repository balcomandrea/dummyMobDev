ONLINE SURVEY TOOL WITH INTERPRETER FOR HOLY CROSS DAVAO COLLEGE LIBRARY USERS

Mutsiola R. Gelmo,
Maureen M. Villamor


ABSTRACT

  The study proposed to established an online survey tool with interpreter that is capable of both gathering data ad interpreting the results to determine the level of satisfaction of Holy Cross of Davao College (HCDC) library users. A third party software called LimeSurvey was customized as fit for an online survey. The online survey is intended to replace the existing paper-pencil survey to be administered by academic librarians to the four academic libraries of HCDC namely; the interpreter is capable of accepting single spreadsheet file uploads saved in Excel which was exported from the online survey. The four surveys created were provided as links to the learning resource Center Web page of the Holy Cross of Davao College Website.
