Aimms' Meat and More POS/Inventory System

Develop by: Lovely Roze N. Amandy, Mice Dianne Morta, April Grace Vergara, Dems Rey Roca
Hermoso Tupas Jr., Nancy Mozo


ABSTRACT

  The factor that drives us to propose Aimm’s meat and more POS/Inventory system is their manual checking of their products left and monitoring inflows and outflows sale of their products, and lastly, their lack of POS system.

  The main objective of this study is to develop an automated system for Aimm’s Meat and more in order to help them in their sales monitoring and billing transactions with an analysis of their sales.

1 of 2

 &emspThe scope of this project is focus on the design and deployment of a Point of Sale system for Aim’s Meat and More. This project consists of products inventory and processing the data of outflow and inflow of sales. Also, this project will have generated sales information that will provide statistics of the store’s sales the users of the system can either be the store owner or the assigned cashier or staff.
